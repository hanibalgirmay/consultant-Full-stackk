MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING for Partnership

On the Establishment of a Partnership between AAUBE, OO, Center for Consultancy, Research & Training/CRT Service & .to Foster Collaboration and Joint Venture Business Engagement

Background:

AAUBE was established by Addis Ababa University, AAU and Addis Ababa University Workers Saving & Credit Limited Liability Cooperative Society, AAUWSCLLCS on Jan 2014, with the aim to engage in different business & investment activities in order to generate financial resource that would eventually enable AAU to achieve its long term aim for self-reliance. Therefore, having Organizational Capacity & Legal Personality to undertake CRT services using AAU’s professionals from diversified disciplines can make a difference, AAUBE is looking for partnership to provide and deliver quality and need based services for Clients. This MoU is framed in a such way:

1. General:

The below listed points are among the major reason for looking partnership for synergy to work in partnership arises by taking into consideration

  1. Having regard business development and service giving organizations are individual based and working with limited capacity;
  2. Having regard the shortage of well organized, credible and competitive institute in the area of education, training, research, consultancy and publication that accelerate the effort and work of those sectors;
  3. Having regard the need and outsourcing of various works are flowing on the market but still there is unmet needs and quality of works are not yet delivered;
  4. Having regard to the professionals and experts are not coming together and contribute their best in the development of the sector;
  5. Having regard different consultancy, research & training works are owned by few with the limitation in organizational capacity though still there is expertise and competencies in the local market available;
  6. Having some of the areas like Consultancy, Research, Training and educational & research based publication related activities are not yet untapped.
  7. Having IGOs, Public Sectors, INGOs, NGOs, and the private sector is wasting their time and scarce resource in search of professionals to do the work together;
  8. Having regard there is no so much formal partnership exists and such culture is not yet well developed;
  9. Having regard still there is a positive trend, a growing prospect and a moral obligation to respond to all these professional matters;
  10. Believing that all these things should be met and the challenge can be resolved through partnership, collaboration and joint venture; Therefore, AAUBE is looking for partnership.

2. Objective of MoU

2.1. General Objective
  1. Through partnership, AAUBE expresses its wish to form collaboration, cooperation, and joint venture in core areas of the company and bridge the gap prevailed in the area.
  2. The overall objective of the partnership is to strengthen the institutional ties between AAUBE and the partner in the area of CRT and publication of educational & research based items.
2.2. The specific objectives:
  1. Increase understanding on the bases of mutual benefit and professional contribution that will function both parties;
  2. Avail expertise; provide CRT services, material production & publication when the need arises;
  3. Foster better collaboration through effective and wise use of resource between the institutions;
  4. Guarantee timely provision of services, quality of work and deliverables.

3. Partnership Modality

The objectives of the partnership will be achieved through:

  1. Exchange of services: consultancy, research and training services as well as research and educational material development and production
  2. Staff or expert secondment,
  3. Organizing Workshop & Conference;
  4. Certification program;

4. Principles

The partnership will be based on the following general principles:

  1. Respect and mutual understanding;
  2. Respecting organizational structures, mandates and institutional capacities of both parties;
  3. Quality of work, timely deliverables, reasonable cost.
  4. Strong professional and ethical dealings.
  5. Reciprocity of action and commitment.

5. Areas Of Cooperation

5.1 Consultancy
  1. Strategic Plan Development & Management;
  2. Organizational restructuring and changes management;
  3. Salary scale and benefit packages;
  4. Human Resources and Organizational Development;
  5. Human resource planning and management;
  6. Feasibility & Business Development ;
  7. Project Management
  8. Monitoring, Evaluation, Reporting and Learning
  9. Agro-Industry & Manufacturing;
  10. Change Management;
  11. Conflict Management;
  12. Edutainment and Catering services;
  13. Social responsiveness & accountability
  14. Gender and development
  15. E-Governance;
  16. ICT
5.2 Research
  1. A nation-wide researches on socio-economic, governance & politics and Business;
  2. Nation-wide feasibility study;
  3. Evaluation & Studies: Surveillance studies, Baseline, Midterm & Terminal evaluations;
  4. Socio-economic research
  5. ICT and related;
  6. Education & Capacity Building;
  7. Health and Hygiene;
  8. Culture/Traditional Practices & Values;
5.3 Training:
  1. Open/Regular Training categorized in Management Development Training & Expertise Development Training;
  2. Tailor-made Training based on the TNA of the Client’s Organization;
5.4 Secondmnet:
  1. Professionals’ deployment at IGOs, INGOs, Multinationals Cooperation, etc;
  2. Overseas deployment of professionals;
5.5 Certification:

Certification Program with a short term training

5.6 Publication:

Educational & Research based Material Development & Publication such as: Training material, Books, leaflets, booklets, posters, radio and TV spot programs, Magazines;

6 Financial Framework:

  1. Both parties shall stand for the Transparent, reliable & timely documentation of transaction;
  2. The enterprise administration & finance department shall be entitled to manage the finance as far as the Enterprise is going to earn 50 %& above from the delivery of a service delivered as per this partnership;
  3. Depending on the nature of the intended CRT service, the percentage of partner share, the level of partners engagement, the structure & capacity of the Finance Unit of the partner, the partner finance unit might manage the finance;

7 Benefits Of Partnership:

  1. Synergy For Mutual Success
  2. Goodwill & Facility Sharing
  3. Quality Service Provision With Guarantee.
  4. Simplifying Bid Process And Complications.
  5. Confidence And Trust Building
  6. Timely Deliverables
  7. Competent And Well Experienced.

8. Duration & Termination

This MoU is effective until the life span of a strategic plan years/ 5 Years. Both parties shall establish a comprehensive agreement upon securing the Tender/Assignment.
